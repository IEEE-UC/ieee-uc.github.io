IEEE Student Magazine

The IEEE Student Magazine is a student-led magazine that publishes research, projects, and experiences done by UC students. We accept articles from graduate students and undergraduate students that cover passionate personal projects, outstanding coursework, and international, national, or local conferences. Unsure if your article fits these categories? Message the executive board in our Discord server and we’ll let you know!

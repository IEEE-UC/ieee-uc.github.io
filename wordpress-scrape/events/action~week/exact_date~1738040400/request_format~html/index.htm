Events

Check back in periodically for links to previous meeting’s slides and follow-up information from the speakers!

IEEE speakers are typically invited to give their presentations during regularly scheduled meetings (alternating Thursdays at 5 pm). Meetings are usually—unless otherwise noted—held in room 3220 of Campus Rec and last 45 to 60 minutes.
